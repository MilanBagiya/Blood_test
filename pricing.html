Want to know more?

Health scores combine up to 30 factors about your health, like your sleep quality, diet, and results, into one concise number. Your scores can help you get a clear picture of how your lifestyle is affecting 3 key areas of your health — energy, mental wellbeing, and long-term health.

Using machine learning (a type of artificial intelligence), we can now look at the relationship between over 800,000 test results and health and lifestyle factors. This lets us predict your results, with a 95% chance that your true blood results will fall in the predicted range — includes cholesterol, liver function, iron, and HbA1c (diabetes indicator). This feature has been created by our team of data scientists and NHS doctors. We also work closely with regulatory bodies like the MHRA to make sure everything we do is safe. Learn more about predicted results
